NOUNS

A nounrepresents a person, place, animal, thing or an idea.

KINDS OF NOUN

Nouns are classified as follows:

Phoper:It is the name of a nparticular object, whether person, place, animal,idea or thing.

Qommon:It is the name representing a group of thing.

Abstract:It is the name representing qualities, emotions, conditions or actions.

Pronouns

A pronoun is a word used in place of a noun. A pronoun is generally used to avoid repetition of a noun.
